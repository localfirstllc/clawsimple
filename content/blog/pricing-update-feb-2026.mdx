When we launched [ClawSimple](/), our goal was simple: make powerful agent technology accessible to non-technical users. We started with a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) approach to test the waters.

Over time, thanks to your feedback and support, we **evolved**. ClawSimple is no longer just an experiment; it's becoming a robust, professional platform for autonomous agents.

## Our Journey & Evolution

We haven't just been maintaining; we've been building:

-   **Model Switching**: Flexibility to choose the right intelligence for your tasks at any time, directly inside the Telegram bot.
-   **Transparent and Flexible Billing**: A clear pricing model that adjusts based on the model you choose, giving you full control over costs.
-   **Enhanced Reliability**: Strengthening our core infrastructure for 24/7 enterprise-grade availability.

Unlike many competitors who use shared environments that can be slow or insecure, we have stuck to our commitment of providing **dedicated, isolated environments secured by one-time passwords** for every user. This ensures your data is safe and your agents perform consistently.

## Pricing Updates

To sustain this level of quality and continue developing professional-grade features, we are adjusting our pricing for **new subscriptions** starting **February 15th, 2026 (UTC 00:00)**.

The new pricing structure will be:

-   **Bring Your Own Model (BYOM)**: $9.90 → **$14.90** / month
-   **Standard Plan**: $14.90 → **$24.90** / month
-   **Max Plan**: $34.90 / month


## Legacy Price Protection

We value our early adopters.

**If you have an active subscription before February 15th, 2026, your price will remain locked at the current rate as long as your subscription stays active.**

This is our way of saying thank you for believing in us from the start.

## The Future

This change allows us to invest more into the platform, bringing you faster updates, more advanced tools, and even better support. We are excited about what's coming next for ClawSimple.
